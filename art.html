Art

I've never been terribly consistent about making art, but now and then I get in the mood to draw up some of my characters. Click on any of the images below for a close-up.

Besides character art, I also sometimes make abstract art, paintings, jewelry, and whatever else catches my interest. If you like, you can check out my art site, Neitherwise, to see more. I also have a Redbubble shop where you can get some of my designs printed on just about anything.
